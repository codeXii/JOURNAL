About Us

  • Who We Are: We are Africans first and last. We are a collective of Pan Africans poised to move Pan-Africanism beyond the confines of the colonial nation-state and its political rhetoric to people-based Global Black praxis, for the rejuvenation of Africa and uplift of the living condition of Africans the world over.
  • Our Vision: The Centre aspires to bring about a more egalitarian society to the comity of human nations through interaction, interrogation, etc
  • Our Mission Our Mission is to create an enabling environment to foster free and uninhibited intellectual conversation, political discussion and social engagement.
